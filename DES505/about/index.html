Our Story

Vision

Gentle Words exists to create a safe and comforting space for people across generations. Many people grow up learning religion through strict or outdated interpretations. Our vision is to help people reconnect with their religion in a way that feels supportive, understanding, and healing. We believe that faith should bring comfort, not fear.

Mission

Our mission is to bridge the gap between religion and mental health.

  • Reframing harmful phrases into compassionate ones
  • Sharing meaningful quotes from religious texts
  • Encouraging mindfulness and emotional awareness

We help people keep their faith while also caring for their mental and emotional health.

Overview

Gentle Words is a platform that provides faith-based comfort and reflection. We combine religious teachings, emotional support, and calming design to create a safe and personal experience.

Our Story

This project began with a simple realization: Many people were told to “just pray” or “be stronger” when they were struggling. While these words may come from a place of belief, they can sometimes feel dismissive or isolating.

Gentle Words was created to change that experience. To show that religion can also be gentle, understanding, and emotionally supportive. The teddy bear represents comfort. For many, it is a symbol of safety, warmth, and reassurance from childhood. We use this symbol to bring that same feeling into adulthood. Each bear includes a small tag with a quote from a religious text, offering a moment of reflection, comfort, and peace. It can be placed: By your bedside On your desk Near a personal or spiritual space A gentle reminder of the comfort found in faith.

What We Offer

  • Monthly Teddy Bear Subscription — A comforting item paired with meaningful quotes
  • Faith-Based Reflection Messages — Gentle reminders rooted in religious teachings
  • Reframing Harmful Phrases — Turning negative or dismissive words into supportive ones
  • Educational Resources — Helping people better understand the emotional side of faith

Gentle Words is not about changing religion. It is about changing how we communicate and understand it.

We aim to create more empathy in conversations, more awareness in communities, and more support for those struggling. Because faith and mental health should work together, not against each other.
