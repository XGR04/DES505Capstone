Faith, Reflection, and Gentle Support

Words can stay with us. So we choose to make them gentler.

A $5 monthly subscription includes a teddy bear with a rephrased message to encourage softer thinking while also contributing to a charitable cause of your choice.

PURPOSE

Gentle Words exists to reframe those moments. Through small, intentional changes in language, we create space for understanding instead of pressure, and care instead of silence.

And with every teddy, a portion goes toward a charitable cause — extending that care beyond you.

How It Works

The subscription provides a monthly reminder through gentle messages and reflections to help people heal, grow, and be kinder to themselves.

Create an account and subscribe to receive a monthly teddy bear and access to more of the website's resources.

Receive a teddy bear with a tag that discusses the weekly phrase and a faith-based reflection.

Reflect, learn, and use the website for deeper context and resources.
